Authors: Kwasi Buansi & Pacis Nkubito

Original data: Kaggle Dataset ·

Cleaned Data: View data table

Instructions:

  • Each tick represents an hour in the 24-hour format (military time). Read clockwise.
  • Each bar represents the average orders for that hour, split into two sub-bars by locale (urban vs. suburban).
  • Hover over a sub-bar to see the 12-hour clock conversion, and the average number of orders for that locale.
  • The area of the sub-bar represents the average number of orders for that locale.
  • Click a bar for details.
